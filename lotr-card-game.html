Rules of the One Ring

🎴 The Deck: Each faction commands 13 unique tarot-inspired cards. Draw 5 at start; draw 1 each turn.


💎 Mana: You gain +1 Mana each turn (max 10). Cards cost mana equal to their blue circle number.


⚔️ Playing Cards: Select a card from your hand and click "Play Card" to summon it to your battlefield.


🗡️ Attacking: Select your field card, then click an enemy card (or the enemy hero directly) to attack.


🛡️ Combat: Attacker's ATK vs Defender's DEF. Excess damage hits HP. When a card's HP reaches 0, it's destroyed.


🌟 Special Abilities: Each card has a unique ability shown in gold text. Effects trigger on play or attack.


💀 Victory: Reduce the enemy Hero HP to 0, or force them to draw with no cards remaining.


🤖 The Enemy: An AI opponent will play their cards strategically. Watch for their patterns!
